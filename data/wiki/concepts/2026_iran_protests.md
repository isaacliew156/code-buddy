# 2026 Iran Protests and Massacres

## Overview
The 2026 Iran protests were massive nationwide demonstrations that erupted in December 2025, eventually leading to a brutal government crackdown that killed thousands and precipitated international intervention.

## Timeline

### Protests Begin (December 2025)
- **December 28, 2025**: Massive protests erupt across all 31 Iranian provinces
- **Scale**: Estimated 5 million participants - largest since 1979 revolution
- **Causes**: Initially economic grievances (60% inflation, economic collapse), quickly escalated to calls for overthrow of Islamic Republic

### International Response (January 2026)
- **January 2, 2026**: President Trump threatens military intervention if Iranian forces kill peaceful protesters
- **January 8-10, 2026**: Iranian government imposes near-total internet blackout
- **Massacre**: Security forces open fire on protesters with live ammunition

### Key Events of Crackdown
- **Rasht Bazaar Massacre**: Security forces trap protesters in bazaar, set it on fire, kill those attempting to escape or surrender
- **Hospital crisis**: Hospitals enter crisis mode nationwide
- **Mass arrests**: Over 51,000 people arrested
- **Official acknowledgment**: January 12, 2026 - Iran's Foreign Ministry confirms security forces shot directly at protesters

## Casualty Estimates
- **HRANA**: 7,007 documented deaths
- **Iranian health officials**: Over 30,000 deaths (cited by Trump at State of the Union)
- **Scale**: Death toll among largest in modern Iranian history

## International Reactions
- **US**: Threat of military intervention
- **EU**: Designated IRGC as terrorist organization in response
- **Ukraine**: Also designated IRGC as terrorist organization
- **Economic**: Venezuela's Maduro captured, disrupting Iran-Venezuela sanctions evasion

## Economic Context
- **World Bank**: Projected negative growth for Iran in 2025 and 2026
- **Inflation**: Approaching 60% annually
- **Currency collapse**: Iranian rial collapsed in December 2025
- **Sanctions**: UK, France, Germany triggered reimposition of UN sanctions in September 2025

## Significance
- **Catalyst for war**: Protests and government response created pretext for US intervention
- **International legitimacy**: Brutal crackdown eroded Iran's international standing
- **Domestic instability**: Revealed deep regime vulnerability despite military power

## Aftermath
- **Regime survival**: Government survived but with severe damage to legitimacy
- **War pretext**: Used by US to justify Operation Epic Fury
- **Ongoing executions**: Regime accelerated execution of political prisoners throughout war
