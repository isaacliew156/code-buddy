# Operation Epic Fury

## Overview
Operation Epic Fury is the joint US-Israel large-scale military operation against Iran launched on February 28, 2026. The operation began as a surprise attack targeting Iran's military infrastructure, nuclear facilities, air defenses, and leadership.

## Key Events
- **February 28, 2026**: Operation Epic Fury launched
- **Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei killed** in initial strikes along with several senior officials
- Significant civilian casualties reported
- **March 2026**: Continued strikes on Tehran and other Iranian cities
- **April 2026**: War enters 38th day with no ceasefire

## Objectives
The stated US justification for the operation includes:
- Preventing Iran from acquiring nuclear weapons
- Holding the regime accountable for massacring protesters in January 2026
- Securing freedom of navigation through the Strait of Hormuz

## Military Actions
- Daily US-Israeli strikes on Iranian military sites
- Strikes on weapons production facilities
- Attacks on energy infrastructure (petrochemical complexes, gas fields)
- Targeting of IRGC leadership and facilities

## Status
As of April 7, 2026:
- Active combat continues with no ceasefire
- Over 2,076 killed and 26,500 wounded in Iran
- Over 600 schools and education centers hit in Iran
- 6,833 casualties evacuated to Israeli hospitals
