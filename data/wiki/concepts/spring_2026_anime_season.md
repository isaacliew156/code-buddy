# Spring 2026 Anime Season

Spring 2026 is considered one of the most stacked anime seasons in recent memory, featuring a mix of major returning franchises and highly anticipated new adaptations.

## Top Anime of Spring 2026

### Re:Zero — Starting Life in Another World Season 4
- **Studio**: White Fox
- **Episodes**: 19 (split-cour)
- **Premiere**: April 8, 2026 (Crunchyroll)
- **Status**: Most anticipated anime of Spring 2026 by fan vote
- **Story**: Adapts Desert Tower arc following Subaru's journey to recover stolen name and memories
- **Split**: Loss Arc (11 episodes, April-June) and Recapture Arc (8 episodes starting August 12)
- **Rating**: 8.24/10 MAL user score (second-highest rated isekai)

### Dorohedoro Season 2
- **Studio**: MAPPA
- **Premiere**: April 1, 2026 (3-episode premiere)
- **Status**: Major surprise return after six-year wait
- **Story**: Continues Caiman's search for sorcerer who cursed him with lizard head

### Witch Hat Atelier
- **Studio**: BUG FILMS
- **Director**: Ayumu Watanabe
- **Status**: Most anticipated new adaptation of season
- **Source**: Kamome Shirahama's Eisner Award-winning manga
- **Story**: Coco discovers magic is drawn with special ink, becomes apprentice witch

### That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Season 4
- **Studio**: 8bit
- **Status**: Top of Animate Times poll as Japan's most anticipated
- **Tracking**: 111,270 MAL members tracking it

### Classroom of the Elite Season 4
- **Studio**: Lerche
- **Tracking**: 109,091 MAL members tracking

### Steel Ball Run: JoJo's Bizarre Adventure (Continuing)
- **Studio**: David Production
- **Platform**: Netflix
- **Premiere**: March 19, 2026 (continuing into Spring)
- **Status**: Adaptation of beloved seventh part of manga
